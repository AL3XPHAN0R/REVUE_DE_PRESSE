# Persona & Target Audience (Layer 3 — Reference)

## Author
Alexandre Phanor — future personal insurance broker (vie, invalidité, maladie) in Canada.
Currently building a podcast that demystifies personal insurance for entrepreneurs.
Primary market: Quebec and Canada (bilingual, French-first).

## Target audience
- Canadian SME owners (2–50 employees)
- Self-employed professionals and freelancers
- Entrepreneurs without group benefits
- Business owners exposed to key-person risk
- Founders in early-stage companies (no safety net)

## Relevance criteria — INCLUDE articles about
- Disability insurance / income protection for the self-employed
- Life insurance for business owners and key-person coverage
- Critical illness insurance for entrepreneurs
- Group benefits for small businesses
- AMF / CIRO / Chambre de l'assurance regulation (Quebec and Canada)
- Insurtech and fintech trends affecting SME coverage
- Concrete financial risk scenarios (owner illness, death, burnout)
- SME financial fragility and protection gaps
- Statistics on coverage gaps among Canadian entrepreneurs
- Podcast-worthy angles: stories, case studies, surprising data

## Exclusion criteria — EXCLUDE articles about
- General politics with no insurance angle
- B2C mass market insurance (auto, home, travel)
- Generic macroeconomic news (inflation, interest rates) unless tied to SME risk
- Corporate / enterprise insurance (>500 employees)
- Topics with no clear relevance to personal insurance or SME protection
